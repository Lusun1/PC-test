Mount Recoater Blade

Before start build, there are several preparation steps: Mount the recoater blade, mount and level the building platform, clean lens and double check all set up conditions. In this chapter, we will introduce how to mount the recoater blade. The recoater blade is important in the building process, since it helps the recoater apply the bed of powder evenly.

    There are 3 different types of recoater blades. According to different condition, you need to select different recoater blades to mount.
  1. HSS or High Speed Steel blade
  2. Brush recoater blade
  3. Ceramic recoater blade

HSS or High Speed Steel is used most often, tough blade that will hold up a long time.


Brush recoater blade is used on thin wall or delicate builds so the soft brushes go across the build instead of a hard steel blade.


Ceramic recoater blade is used if the powder has a magnetic charge to it. The magnetic powder will not stick to the ceramic

        Descriptions:

        There are three steps to mount recoater blade:
        Step1. Select the correct recoater blade and ride the recoater blade on recoater housing.
        Step2. Place blade holder on recoater housing.
        Step3. Find screw and screw the blade holder.
