PPE

Before using EOS, there are basic prerequisite about safety:

  • Only qualified persons trained by EOS are allowed to operate the machine
  • It is necessary to wear personal protective equipment(PPE) to prevent hazards.

PPE is for personal protection equipment. The personal protective equipment protects the operator as well as possible against the remaining hazards.

The following items belong to PPE:

  1. Safety glasses
  2. Nitrile gloves
  3. Respirators
  4. Closed toe shoes
  5. Protecting clothing(flame-retardant)
We will introduce each PPE and their functions in the next few slides.

Nitrile gloves:
Function: Protect the hands against skin contact with metal powders.

Safety glasses:
Function: Protect the eyes against the entry of metal powder.

Respirator:
Respirator needed if powder is being used.
Function: Protect against inhaling metal powder dusts and burns

Close toe shoes:
Function: Protect again crushing and falling parts. Due the protection against electrostatic charging they also protect against sparking.

Closed protective clothing made of flame-retardant material:
Function: Protects against direct skin contact with metal powders as well as burns.
