Set up Conditions

After mounting the recoater blade and leveling the build plate, operators need to set up conditions. There are three main steps to set up before the build: adding powder, clean lens and set build conditions.

Powders used in EOS are hazard. There are several measures to prevent hazards.

After selecting the powder, operators also need to estimate the powder needs for a build. Adding enough powder will prevent the crash in the build.

After operators wire PPE, they should lower the dispenser and collector bin.
Please use the buttons on the screen to lower the dispenser abd collector bin.

After adding the powder and cleaning the lens, operators need to set up the machine. Before parts can be built with the machine, the machine must be prepared necessary activities for the building process.

One important step is to make appropriate chamber environment.

For e-manufacturing using EOS M290 systems, a very wide range of materials is available that cover a wide range of applications. Metal powders approved by EOS are categorised according to their varying potential hazards .Currently there are two categories:

  • Metal powders in category A
  • Metal powders in category B

The potential hazards of category B metal powders are greater than those of category A metal powders.The potential hazards of metal condensate produced as process by-products are greater than the related metal powder in both categories.

Powders could also be categorized as active powders and non-active powders:

  • Reactive: Ti-6Al-4V and AlSi10Mg
  • Non-reactive: Nickel superalloys, Cobalt chrome, Stainless steel, and Maraging steel

The varying potential hazards result in differences in the safe handling of the metal powder and in the safe operation of the machine and accessories:

    Cleaning equipment
  • During the cleaning of the machine, the accessories and the working environment, the item of cleaning equipment approved for the related type of metal powder (Vacuum cleaner or Wet separator) must be used.
  • On changing the filter and the particle collecting bin on the Recirculating filter system as well as during cleaning work on the Recirculating filter system, it is only allowed to use the Wet separator.
    Operation of the machine - selection of inert gas
  • The stipulated inert gas must be selected for processing the related type of metal powder.
    Operators should wear PPE
  • Wear PPE, Grounded, Wrist Band, Shoes, or Boot Strap.

How to calculate the mass of powder?

Volume of the build tank or job box (mm3) = length * width * height of the part
Mass (kg) = Volume * density of the solid
Consider the losses in chamber and job box depending on the process you are working with.
Eg: If you have to deposit a build, the height of the build is h (mm). The length of build tank is l (mm), the width of build tank is w (mm), the density of the solid is D (kb mm-3) consider the losses in chamber and job box is x(kg)
Answer: wlhD+x (kg)
Note: We use the height of the buid, not tank, and the length of width of build tank to estimate the powder. In addition, the losses of powder need to be considered.

In order to make appropriate chamber environment, we need to making available an adequate amount of appropriate inert gas. The inert gas could be Argon or Nitrogen.

    Function of inert gas

  • To avoid contamination of the parts during the build, such as oxygen pickup, that affects the material properties for some materials
  • To reduce the presence of oxygen. Reactive materials can catch fire resulting in an explosion in the presence of oxygen.
