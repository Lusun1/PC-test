Leveling Building Platform

After mounting the recoater blade, next preparation step is to leveling the building plate. The build will tilt or broke if the building plate is not flat. If the height of building plate is not correct, the recoater on the machine will crash. In this chapter, you will learn how to level the building platform.

Step1: put the build plate on the building platform.

Step2: pick the bolts to tight.

Step3: low the build plate to make sure that when you move the recoater above the build plate, it will not crash with the build plate.

Step4: put the build plate on the building platform.

Step5: pick the indicator on the recoater housing

Step6: Move the Indicator back and forth in Y-axis

Step7: Adjust Level Rocker Switches until the Indicator's value is stable while moving

Step8: In the real operation, you need to close the Process Chamber door in order to move the recoater from left to the right. But in our simulation, you could press the button on the screen to move the recoater left or right.

Step9: Adjust Level Rocker Switches until the Indicator's value is stable while moving.

Step10: Find 0.55mm Feeler Gauges to check the distance between Recoater and Buld Plate.

Step11: remove indicator.

HSS or High Speed Steel is used most often, tough blade that will hold up a long time.


Brush recoater blade is used on thin wall or delicate builds so the soft brushes go across the build instead of a hard steel blade.


Ceramic recoater blade is used if the powder has a magnetic charge to it. The magnetic powder will not stick to the ceramic
